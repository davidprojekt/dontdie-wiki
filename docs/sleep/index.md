# Sleep — The Most Powerful Longevity Drug

Sleep is not just rest; it is your body’s most powerful tool for repair, regeneration, and longevity.  
Bryan Johnson emphasizes disciplined, high-quality sleep as the cornerstone of his "Blueprint" approach to extended healthspan.

In this module, we cover everything you need to know about sleep: how much to get, how to improve quality, how to track it, and how to troubleshoot common problems.

## Why Sleep Matters
- Regulates metabolic and inflammatory processes
- Supports cellular repair and immune function
- Enhances cognitive function and memory
- Directly impacts mortality risk
